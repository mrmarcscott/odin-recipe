Boiled Chicken

A recipe for boiled chicken; boiled chicken meat is called for in many other recipes, and is a wonderful basic recipe to have for many uses.

A wonderful easy recipe you can do for your Sunday night dinners with the family. This won';t break the bank, and is sure to have the family begging for more.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs chicken
  • 1 lg onion, halved-unpeeled
  • 3 carrots, cut into chunks-unpeeled
  • 2 stalks celery, cut into chunks
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • water to cover

Directions

  1. Place chicken in a large pot with onion, carrots, celery and peppercorns; add water to cover. Cover pot and bring to a boil; reduce heat to a gentle boil and cook for about 90 minutes,
    or until chicken meat is falling off of the bone. Remove chicken, let cool and shred or chop the meat.
